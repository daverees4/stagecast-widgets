Inside the Score: Berg's Lyric Suite

The Tana String Quartet and soprano Julia Sitkovetsky join writer and broadcaster Gavin Plumley to explore Alban Berg’s Lyric Suite for string quartet and soprano in this visually and musically illustrated lecture-recital.

Composed in 1926 the work was Berg’s love letter to his mistress, Hanna Fuchs. As dramatic as any opera, the quartet features various numerical, musical and textual codes, including a song, concealed within its final movement.

This lecture-recital, accompanied by projections from the stage, will read between the ledger lines and unlock the secrets behind one of the Second Viennese School’s most passionate scores.
